What is the structure of your dataset?¶

The dataset has 14,222 records with 23 features. Most of the features are categorical and several are ordinal.

What is/are the main feature(s) of interest in your dataset?¶

This analysis seeks to understand the nature of conflict in Brazil by investigating fatality counts, locations and event type of over 14,000 events that took place between Jan 1, 2019 and May 4, 2020.

What features in the dataset do you think will help support your investigation into your feature(s) of interest?¶

The main features in the dataset will be fatalities, location and event_type. More specific sub categories such as event_date, latitude, longitude and sub_event_type will also be useful features.
